Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a free consultation?

You can schedule a free consultation by filling out the contact form on this page, sending us an email, or reaching out via WeChat. We'll respond within 24 hours to arrange a time that works for you.

Can parents join the consultation sessions?

Absolutely! We encourage parents to participate in the initial consultation if desired. We can conduct these sessions in English or Chinese based on your preference.

Where are your coaching sessions conducted?

Our coaching sessions are primarily conducted online via video conferencing, making our services accessible regardless of your location. For clients in certain areas, we may also offer in-person sessions by arrangement.

How quickly can I expect a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please indicate this in your message subject line.
