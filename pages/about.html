About Orienta

Our story, our team, and the values that drive our mission

Our Story

Orienta was born from a deep understanding of the challenges faced by Chinese students pursuing careers in the United States. Our founders experienced firsthand the cultural gaps, communication barriers, and unique pressures that international students navigate when transitioning from academic success to professional fulfillment.

The name "Orienta" reflects our dual mission: honoring Eastern cultural roots while providing orientation and direction in Western professional environments. We believe that successful careers are built not just on skills and knowledge, but on authentic self-expression and cultural confidence.

Our approach is summarized in the words of former U.S. Ambassador Gary Locke: "One mile. Three generations. One hundred years." We help our clients accelerate their journey by bridging cultural understanding and professional opportunity.

Our Values

Honest, ethical guidance is paramount in all our interactions. We prioritize long-term success over quick fixes and maintain transparency in our approach and results.

Your goals and well-being are our top priority. We tailor our coaching to your unique background, aspirations, and challenges, ensuring personalized support throughout your journey.

Excellence

Our Approach

Bicultural Expertise

Our team combines deep understanding of Chinese cultural values with extensive experience in American professional environments. This dual perspective allows us to bridge communication gaps and help you navigate complex cultural dynamics.

Psychological Foundation

We integrate evidence-based psychological assessments and coaching techniques to help you understand your strengths, values, and ideal work environment. This self-awareness forms the foundation for authentic career development.

Practical Skill Development

Beyond theory, we focus on building practical skills that employers value. From communication techniques to problem-solving frameworks, we help you develop capabilities that translate across industries and roles.

Family Integration

We recognize the important role that family plays in career decisions, especially in Chinese culture. Our approach includes parents in appropriate ways, providing them with insights and updates while respecting your independence.
