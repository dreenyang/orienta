Our Services

Comprehensive support for students and parents throughout the career journey

Services for Students

We empower students to make informed decisions about their academic and professional futures. Our personalized coaching helps you navigate the complexities of the American job market with confidence.

Career Exploration & Assessment

Discover your strengths, interests, and ideal career path through comprehensive assessments and guided exploration.

Major & University Selection Guidance

Make informed decisions about your academic path with guidance tailored to your career goals.

Internship & Job Search Strategy

Develop a targeted approach to finding opportunities that align with your skills and aspirations.

Resume & Cover Letter Development

Create compelling application materials that highlight your unique value to potential employers.

Interview Preparation & Mock Interviews

Build confidence and skills through practice interviews with personalized feedback.

Networking Skills & Personal Branding

Learn how to build meaningful professional relationships and develop your personal brand.

Graduate School Application Support

Navigate the graduate school application process with expert guidance and feedback.

Student Service Packages

We offer flexible service packages to meet your specific needs and goals:

  • Career Exploration Package: Assessments, career planning, and major selection guidance
  • Internship Success Package: Resume development, interview prep, and application strategy
  • Full Career Launch Package: Comprehensive support from exploration to job placement
  • Graduate School Preparation: Application strategy, personal statement development, and interview prep

Contact us for detailed pricing information based on your specific needs.

Parent Service Options

We offer several ways for parents to stay involved and informed:

  • Regular Updates: Scheduled updates on your child's progress in Chinese
  • Parent Consultations: One-on-one sessions to address your questions and concerns
  • Family Planning Sessions: Joint meetings with students and parents to align expectations
  • Parent Community: Connect with other parents navigating similar experiences

Parent services can be added to any student package or purchased separately.
