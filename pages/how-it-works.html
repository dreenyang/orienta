Our Coaching Process

A proven step-by-step approach to help Chinese students launch successful careers in the U.S.

The Orienta Method

Our comprehensive approach combines psychological assessment, personalized coaching, and cross-cultural expertise to guide you toward career success.

Initial Consultation

We begin with a free consultation to understand your background, goals, and challenges. During this session, we'll explain our approach, answer questions, and determine if our services are a good fit for your needs.

Both students and parents are welcome to join, and we can conduct sessions in English or Chinese based on your preference.

1

Assessment & Planning

After enrolling, we conduct thorough assessments to identify your strengths, interests, values, and areas for development. We use industry-standard tools including StrengthsFinder and custom psychological assessments.

Based on these insights, we create a personalized coaching plan aligned with your career goals and timeline.

2

One-on-One Coaching

You'll work directly with your dedicated coach through regular sessions focused on developing essential skills and strategies. Topics include resume building, interview preparation, networking techniques, communication skills, and cultural adaptation.

Our coaches have extensive experience in Fortune 500 companies and understand both Chinese and American workplace cultures.

3

Job Application Support

We provide comprehensive support throughout the job search process, from identifying target companies to preparing for interviews. You'll receive guidance on crafting standout applications, navigating online assessments, and developing compelling personal narratives.

For parents, we offer regular updates in Chinese about progress and industry insights.

4

Job Placement

With our success-fee model, we're invested in your outcomes. We leverage our industry connections and expertise to help you secure interviews and offers aligned with your career goals.

Throughout this phase, we provide intensive interview preparation, salary negotiation guidance, and strategic advice on evaluating offers.

5

Ongoing Career Support

Our relationship doesn't end when you land a job. We provide continued coaching to ensure you thrive in your new role and position yourself for future advancement.

Through our Peace-of-Mind Membership, both students and parents can access resources, community support, and professional development opportunities.

6

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the coaching process typically take?

The timeline varies based on your starting point and goals. For students seeking internships, we recommend starting 3-6 months before application season. For full-time roles, 6-12 months of preparation is ideal. Our most comprehensive programs include ongoing support for up to 24 months.

How is the success fee structured?

We charge a base fee for our services plus a success fee that's only collected when you achieve your career goals. The specific structure depends on your program and objectives, which we'll discuss during your consultation.

Do you work with students in all majors and industries?

Yes, we support students across all academic disciplines and career paths. Our coaches have expertise in technology, finance, consulting, healthcare, marketing, and many other fields.

How do you communicate with parents who don't speak English?

We offer dedicated parent communication in Mandarin Chinese through WeChat, email, or phone calls. Parents receive regular updates on their child's progress and can schedule private consultations with our bilingual team members.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our coaching, we'll work with you to address your concerns or provide a partial refund based on our terms of service.
